Project: Customer Retention

Customer Retention Insights
(SQL + Tableau)

Explored churn behavior using SQL to clean and query the Telco dataset, followed by Tableau dashboard design for data storytelling.

❮ ❯

Objective

To understand which customers are more likely to churn and why, based on contract type, tenure, and service features.

Tools Used

  • SQLite: Data cleaning, queries, recoding churn
  • Tableau: Dashboards, filters, tooltips

Insights

  • Month-to-month users have the highest churn rate
  • Fiber internet subscribers are more likely to cancel
  • Customers in their first year of service churn the most

Outcome

Developed a clean and useful dashboard to visualize churn drivers. Practiced SQL for data wrangling and Tableau for storytelling.
