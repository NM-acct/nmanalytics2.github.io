Project: Customer Retention

Customer Retention Insights
(SQL + Tableau)

Explored churn behavior using SQL to clean and query the Telco dataset, followed by Tableau dashboard design for data storytelling.

Tools & Skills

  • SQLite: Querying, cleaning missing data, recoding churn categories
  • Tableau: Interactive visuals on contract type, tenure, and internet services

Findings

  • Month-to-month customers churn more often than those with yearly contracts
  • Fiber internet users show a higher churn rate than DSL users
  • Tenure below 12 months correlates with highest churn risk
