Project: Product Returns

Product Returns Analysis
(Excel + Power Query + Power BI)

This project visualizes product return reasons, quantities, and trends to help optimize return policies and reduce losses.

❮ ❯

The images shown are the dashboard, messy data, and cleaned data, respectively.

View Excel Data on Google Drive

Objective:
  • What are the monthly sales and profit trends?
  • What categories and regions are most profitable?
  • What products are underperforming?
    		•
    Tools Used:
  • Excel: Data cleaning, formulas, pivot tables
  • Power BI: KPIs, charts, segmentation visuals
    		•
    Insights:
  • Most revenue comes from Female members buying Health & Beauty products.
  • Evening and weekend sales perform significantly better.
  • Customer ratings tend to align with payment method preferences.
    		•
    Outcome: Built a clear dashboard that helped simulate real-world analysis using pivot tables and Power BI dashboards. Great for sales trend reporting practice.
