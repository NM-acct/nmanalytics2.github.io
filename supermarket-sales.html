Project: Supermarket Sales

Supermarket Sales Analysis
(Excel + Power BI)

Analyzed supermarket sales data to identify trends and performance indicators across product lines, customer types, and purchase times.

❮ ❯

Objective

To explore sales data by customer type, product line, and time of day, and uncover patterns to help business decisions.

Tools Used

  • Excel: Data cleaning, formulas, pivot tables
  • Power BI: KPIs, charts, segmentation visuals

Insights

  • Most revenue comes from Female members buying Health & Beauty products
  • Evening and weekend sales perform significantly better
  • Customer ratings tend to align with payment method preferences

Outcome

Built a clear dashboard that helped simulate real-world analysis using pivot tables and Power BI dashboards. Great for sales trend reporting practice.
